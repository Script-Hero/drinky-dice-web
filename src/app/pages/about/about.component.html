What is Drinky Dice?

Drinky Dice is a fun, dice based drinking game. Every player starts off with one six-sided dice, and they all roll. The player who rolled the lowest number drinks, and then receives another dice. The players roll again, and the players with multiple die add all of their die together.

Why add more die?

Giving losing players more die means while there is always a chance that anyone will have to drink on any given roll, it increases the odds that winning players will drink and decreases the odds that losing players will have to drink. Once everyone has had the same number of drinks, the chance of drinking will be equal for everyone.
